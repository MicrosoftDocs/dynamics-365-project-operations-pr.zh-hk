<span data-ttu-id="3a60f-104">_**適用於：** 資源/非庫存型案例適用的 Project Operations、精簡部署 - 交易至開立預估發票_</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3a60f-104">_**Applies To:** Project Operations for resource/non-stocked based scenarios, Lite deployment - deal to proforma invoicing_</span></span>
